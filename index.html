ABOUT US

UCLA Dragon Boat is a student-run competitive club sports team. Our team and its members embody the spirit of competition and excellence. We are 50 paddlers strong, making up two race crews: Blue and Gold.

For the past 15 years, the team has demonstrated a strong sense of enthusiasm and spirit for the sport. THrough hard work and dedication, our team not only helps its members develop as paddlers, but also as students, athletes, and leaders. Above all else, UCLA Dragon Boat strives to be competitive, passionate, and fun.
